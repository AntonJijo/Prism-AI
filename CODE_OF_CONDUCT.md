# Code of Conduct

## Our Goal

PRISM AI aims to foster a respectful, professional, and collaborative open-source environment.

---

# Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior:
- respectful communication
- constructive feedback
- professionalism
- collaboration
- inclusive participation

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:
- harassment
- hate speech
- discrimination
- intimidation
- malicious disruption
- abusive conduct

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation when necessary.

---

# Community Direction

The PRISM AI community is focused on:
- engineering excellence
- responsible AI development
- open collaboration
- long-term innovation
